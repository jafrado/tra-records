Requirements for TRA Approved GPS Altitude Reporting devices

  • GPS Altitude Reporting device is manufactured by an Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM).
    • OEM is accountable for the design and implementation of the product and support of the same.
    • Device/Unit needs to be available for purchase from an OEM as a completed and verified system.
  • OEM Unit uses and provides documented GPS chipset manufactured by a GPS chipset OEM.
  • OEM shall make the GPS chipset device datasheet public
  • For the OEM GPS chipset, it is mandatory to have a documented 3D dynamic mode and mandatory that this mode is enabled otherwise the altitude data is not trustworthy.
    • The performance and operational limits of the GPS chipset (or receiver core) shall be provided from the manufacturer datasheet.
  • Native GPS chipset output (e.g. NMEA, UBX, GPX, Proprietary, etc) is required (e.g. a GPS logfile).
    • In the absence of native GPS logfile output, OEM Device/Unit shall provide minimum required data (MRD), as below in CSV format:
      • Latitude, Longitude, Altitude (LLA)
      • GPS Dilution of Precision (DOP) or Satellites In View (SIV)
      • Fix-Type,Time-UTC and GNSS constellation information is encouraged and desirable but not required.
    • At least three (3) datafiles from three (3) flights of 10K-80Kft that compares with barometric or GPS data is required to qualify.
  • All ITAR and export limits for the specified GPS chipset OEM shall be followed.
    • Modification of OEM GPS chipset firmware binary images or HW behaviour is forbidden.
    • Tampering, modifying, or circumventing features or normal operational modes of the OEM GPS chipset in HW or SW is forbidden.

Approved OEM GPS Chipsets and their Operational Limits

  • uBlox NEO-M8N, NEO-M7 , LEA-4,LEA-5,LEA-6
    • Aircraft Dynamic Model: 4g, Mach 1.5 Horizontal & Vertical (500 m/s), 164,042’ MSL (50km) max altitude
  • uBlox NEO-M9N
    • Aircraft Dynamic Model: 4g, Mach 60 Vertical (20km/sec), Mach 1.5 Horizontal (500 m/s), 262,467’ MSL (80km) max altitude
  • Trimble Lassen-IQ
    • 4g, Mach 1.5 Horizontal and Vertical (500 m/s), 59,055' MSL (18km) max altitude
  • Novatel OEM-615V/OEM-7
    • 20g, Mach 1.5 Horizontal and Vertical (515 m/s) 50km (OEM)
    • Unlimited speed and velocity (with license and unlock code)
